Mango Mule

What we will be making

Fresh summer classic with ginger beer and mango for a twist served in a copper cup minus the vodka

Ingredients

  • 4-5 slices of cucumber
  • 1 oz honey syrup
  • 1.5 oz mango puree
  • 1.5 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1.5 oz ginger beer
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Muddle cucumber and honey syrup in the bottom of a cocktail shaker
  2. Add the mango puree and lime juice and shake with ice igorously
  3. Strain into copper mug
  4. Top with ginger beer and stir
