Monster Cookies

What we will be making

Chocolatey, chewy, peanut buttery goodness. There's no better way to describe this tried and tested family recipe for Monster Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Sugar
  • 1 cup of packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups peanut butter
  • 1 TB vanilla extract
  • 2 ts baking soda
  • 1/2 ts salt
  • 4 1/2 cups oats
  • 1 cup mini M&Ms
  • 1 cup mini-chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper
  2. Mix together sugar, brown sugar and butteruntil light and fluffy
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, and then beat in the peanut butter, vanilla, baking soda and salt until well combined
  4. Add the oats and mix until combined,then add the mini M&Ms and mini-chocolate chips
  5. Scoop 3 tablespoon mounds of the dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing the mounds at least 2 inches apart
  6. Bake the cookies for 12-15 minutes, then let cool for 5 minutes.
  7. ENJOY!
